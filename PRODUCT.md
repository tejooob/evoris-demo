# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective dental patients in Kharghar / Navi Mumbai (families, working professionals) searching for a trustworthy implant and root-canal specialist nearby. They arrive from Google Business Profile or word of mouth, mostly on mobile, and want to (a) feel the clinic is premium and credible, (b) see the doctors' credentials, (c) find timings and call in one tap.

Secondary audience: the clinic's own doctors (Dr. Shashank Deshpande, Dr. Shivani Vyavahare Deshpande), who are evaluating this demo to decide whether to commission a full multi-page site.

## Product Purpose

Single-page demo website for Evoris Dental Care & Implant Center (Shop No. 3, Vasundhara-II CHS, Sector 8, Kharghar, Navi Mumbai 410210; phone 9137161693). Demo scope only; full site comes after client commitment. Success = the doctor sees it and says "yes, build the full site."

## Brand Personality

Premium, precise, reassuring. The physical signage is the brand: champagne-gold inscriptional lettering on warm ivory, deep navy for the doctors' names, a glowing tooth emblem, plus Devanagari "इवोरिस दंत चिकित्सालय". The site should feel like that sign: gold-on-dark luxury with clinical calm, not startup-blue dentistry.

## Anti-references

- Generic teal-and-white dental templates (Dentee/Practo clone look)
- Stock photo grids of fake grinning models on every section
- Discount-dentistry urgency (offer banners, countdown timers)

## Design Principles

1. The signage is the design system: champagne gold, ivory, deep navy, inscriptional serif.
2. One tap to call: the phone number is the primary conversion everywhere.
3. Credentials build trust: BDS/MDS specialties stated plainly, not buried.
4. Local and real: actual address, actual timings, Devanagari name, map link.
5. Demo-grade polish over breadth: one page that proves the craft.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast, reduced-motion alternatives, semantic HTML, mobile-first (most visitors arrive from Google Maps on phones).
